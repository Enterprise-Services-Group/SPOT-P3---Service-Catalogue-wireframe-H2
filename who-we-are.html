Who we are

The Teaching Lab Support team provides reliable, consistent operational support for teaching and learning activities across the Faculty's laboratories. Each School is supported by a teaching support specialist team that delivers core services and providing technical expertise.

Together, we play a key role in keeping teaching spaces ready, safe, and well-resourced, coordinating support across shared and multi-faculty spaces, and ensuring a consistent experience for staff and students throughout the teaching cycle.

Get to know the team and more information about labs and resources, visiting our Teaching Lab Support SharePoint site.
