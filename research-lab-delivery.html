Delivery framework

There are five stages in our service delivery framework.

1

Lodge request

You identify and lodge a support request with us.

2

Assess and triage

We prioritise and assign your request or provide a helpful handover if we can't assist.

3

Resolve and deliver

We take action to fulfil your request, keeping each other updated on any changes.

4

Close the loop

We confirm when the request is complete with you and others as needed.

5

Review and improve

We capture your feedback to assess our performance and make regular improvements to our offerings.

Channels

Stages 1–4

The preferred channel is email. You can also speak to us on the phone or face-to-face with embedded Lab Tech staff.
